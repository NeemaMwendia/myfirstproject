PARTS OF AN ORCHESTRA

WHAT IS AN ORCHESTRA?

An orchestra is a group of instrumentalists who play a variety of instruments together

Types of instrumeants in an orchestra

    String family

  1. violin
  2. viola
  3. cello
  4. harp
  5. double bass aka. contrabass

    Woodwind family

  1. oboe
  2. flute
  3. saxophone
  4. clarinet
  5. reccorder
  6. bassoon
  7. piccolo

    Brass family

  1. trombone
  2. french horn
  3. trumpet
  4. tuba
  5. cornet
  6. flagelhorn
  7. mellophone
  8. baritone horn

    Percussion family

  1. cymbals
  2. marimba
  3. tambourine
  4. chimes
  5. snare drums
  6. the triangle
  7. bass drum
  8. timpani
  9. xylophone
  10. vibraphone
  11. claves

All instruments in an orchestra work together to make a sound pleasing to the ear

There are several types of orchestras, namely:

  • Full orchestra

    • which is the largest

  • Chamber orchestra

    • which is a smaller version

  • Pit orchestra

    • which is a subset of the full orchestra that provides live music for a play or opera

  • Concert band

    • which is a large wind ensemble
